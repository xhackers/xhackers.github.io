AN OVERNIGHT BLUEMIX HACK

Take a second to think about what distinguishes you as a coder.Is it your impressive ability to stay (semi) functional for 24 hours on end? Or maybe its your unmatched ability to maintain a sense of humor, even when the Gods of Code decide to pick on you that day?

While all of these are impressive, what really distinguishes you coders from others, is your unparalleled PASSION for what you do.
So, it's time to pack your bags and join us for this XHacknight ! Don't forget to bring your laptops !

LEARN FROM EXPERTS

To transform your idea into a mobile app, you definitely need some expert guidance. So, on Sept 5th (Day 1), Starting from 9:30 AM till afternoon, we will have experts training you.
The agenda is

  • Introduction to IBM Bluemix services
  • Introduction to Xamarin platform & Xamarin
  • Code samples walkthrough
  • Revealing the challenges for Hackathon

HACK TO WIN

Do you have an ability to easily process immense amounts of water?
Stay awake till 5 PM on Day 2 and explore your passion that is derived from a single feeling and is guided by your need to see that feeling turn into a thought, the thought turn into an idea and the idea come to life on a mobile phone !

  • Pick one out of the four challenges explained
  • Feel free to reuse the code samples
  • Build atleast for two mobile platforms out of iOS, Android and Windows using IBM Bluemix services
